# Survey instrument - AI in Product Management

Author: Rodrigo Tozato - Produto com IA  
Version: 1.0 - July 22, 2026  
Use: instrument for future data collection; this file contains no results.  
Reuse is permitted with attribution and a link to https://produtocomia.com.br/en/guides/state-of-ai-in-product-management-2026/

## Consent and eligibility

1. Have you worked in product management during the past 12 months?
2. Do you consent to aggregated, anonymous use of responses for editorial research?
3. Country, industry, organization size and seniority.

## AI use

4. Which product tasks used AI during the past 30 days?
5. Frequency by task: never, monthly, weekly, several times a week, daily.
6. Does AI suggest only, or execute actions in other systems?
7. Which tools and models are approved by the organization?

## Value, quality and governance

8. Which task produced the clearest gain, and what evidence supports it?
9. Was time, rework or cost reduced, and how was it measured?
10. Where did quality decline or review increase?
11. Are there rules for confidential data, evaluation, approval and incidents?
12. Who is accountable when AI output affects the product?
13. Which capabilities became more important?
14. What is the largest barrier to responsible value from AI?

Before publishing any number, document collection period, recruitment channels, eligibility, duplicates, valid responses, text processing, sample size and limitations.
