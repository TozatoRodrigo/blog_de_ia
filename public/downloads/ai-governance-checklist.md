# AI governance checklist

Author: Rodrigo Tozato - Produto com IA  
Version: 1.0 - July 22, 2026  
Canonical page: https://produtocomia.com.br/en/guides/ai-governance-checklist/  
Reuse is permitted with attribution and a link to the canonical page.

## Purpose and accountability

- [ ] Problem, user and intended outcome are documented.
- [ ] Non-AI alternatives were considered.
- [ ] Prohibited uses and boundaries are clear.
- [ ] Product and technical owners are named.

## Data, model and vendor

- [ ] Data origin, rights, quality and retention were verified.
- [ ] Personal and confidential data were minimized.
- [ ] Model, version, vendor, region and integrations are recorded.
- [ ] Vendor replacement, outage and exit have a plan.

## Evaluation, launch and operations

- [ ] The system is inventoried and risk-classified.
- [ ] Normal, critical, adversarial and prohibited cases were tested.
- [ ] Thresholds were defined before evaluation.
- [ ] Approval and oversight match impact.
- [ ] Logs, alerts, rollback and incident response were tested.
- [ ] Material changes are versioned.
- [ ] Review and retirement criteria have an owner and date.
